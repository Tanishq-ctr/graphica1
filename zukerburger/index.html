Army Institute Of Technology

Army Institute of Technology is an engineering college located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. It is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. Only wards of army personnel are admitted in this institute. AIT is operated by the Army Welfare Education Society (AWES) and has the Chief of Army Staff of the Indian Army (COAS), as the president of its board of governors.

There are about 23 clubs in AIT PUNE in which there are 3 boards i.e Technical Board, Cultural Board, Sports Board. AIT has a variety of clubs, including academic-focused clubs (coding, robotics, science), cultural clubs (music, dance, drama), sports clubs, and social service clubs.

The coding, robotics, and cultural clubs are among the most active, frequently hosting workshops, competitions, and events. The sports clubs also see active participation.
